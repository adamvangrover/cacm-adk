Meta Reports First Quarter 2025 Results

MENLO PARK, Calif. – April 26, 2025 – Meta Platforms, Inc. (NASDAQ: META) today reported financial results for the quarter ended March 31, 2025.

"We've had a strong start to 2025, with continued growth across our Family of Apps and encouraging developments in our AI and Reality Labs efforts," said Mark Zuckerberg, Meta founder and CEO. "Our investments in AI are enhancing user engagement and monetization, and we are making significant strides in building the foundational platforms for the metaverse."

First Quarter 2025 Financial Highlights
(all comparisons are year-over-year unless otherwise noted)
- Revenue: $48.5 billion, an increase of 20%.
- Net Income: $16.2 billion, an increase of 28%.
- Earnings Per Share (Diluted): $6.00, an increase of 29%.
- Family of Apps (FoA) revenue: $47.8 billion, an increase of 19%.
- Reality Labs (RL) revenue: $0.7 billion, an increase of 15%.
- Headcount: 107,500 as of March 31, 2025, an increase of 2% from December 31, 2024.

First Quarter 2025 Operational and Other Financial Highlights
- Family daily active people (DAP) – DAP was 3.30 billion on average for March 2025, an increase of 8%.
- Facebook daily active users (DAUs) – DAUs were 2.18 billion on average for March 2025, an increase of 7%.
- Advertising revenue grew 19.5% to $47.0 billion.
- The company repurchased $10.5 billion of its Class A common stock in the first quarter.
- Meta expects second quarter 2025 total revenue to be in the range of $49-52 billion.
