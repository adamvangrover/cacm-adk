Meta Reports Fourth Quarter and Full Year 2024 Results

MENLO PARK, Calif. – February 1, 2025 – Meta Platforms, Inc. (NASDAQ: META) today reported financial results for the quarter and full year ended December 31, 2024.

"2024 was a pivotal year for Meta, with strong growth in our Family of Apps and continued progress in our Reality Labs initiatives," said Mark Zuckerberg, Meta founder and CEO. "Our community continues to grow, and we are optimistic about the trajectory of our AI and metaverse investments heading into the new year."

Fourth Quarter and Full Year 2024 Financial Highlights
(all comparisons are year-over-year unless otherwise noted)
- Revenue: Q4 $45.2 billion (+18%); FY $160.5 billion (+15%)
- Net Income: Q4 $15.1 billion (+25%); FY $50.3 billion (+20%)
- Earnings Per Share (Diluted): Q4 $5.50 (+26%); FY $18.00 (+21%)
- Family of Apps (FoA) revenue: Q4 $44.5 billion (+17%); FY $158.0 billion (+14%)
- Reality Labs (RL) revenue: Q4 $0.7 billion (+30%); FY $2.5 billion (+25%)
- Headcount: 105,345 as of December 31, 2024, an increase of 5%.

Full Year 2024 Operational and Other Financial Highlights
- Family daily active people (DAP) – DAP was 3.25 billion on average for December 2024, an increase of 7%.
- Facebook daily active users (DAUs) – DAUs were 2.15 billion on average for December 2024, an increase of 6%.
- Advertising revenue grew 14% to $155.0 billion.
- Capital expenditures, including principal payments on finance leases, were $30.0 billion for the full year 2024.
