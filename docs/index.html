Foundational Pillars: The Semantic Backbone

The platform's success hinges on a robust semantic infrastructure. These pillars are Priority Zero, enabling all advanced capabilities.

üìú

Ontology (`credit_ontology.ttl`)

A "living contract" formally defining shared understanding across the platform. Defines all terms, classes (e.g., `kgclass:FinancialInstrument`), and properties (e.g., `kgprop:hasRating`).

→
üîó

Knowledge Graph (KG)

Instantiated from the ontology, dynamically populated with financial models, decision logic, simulated scenarios, and expert inputs. Enables advanced querying and relationship discovery.

→
üìö

Knowledge Base (KB)

Curated repository of verified, domain-specific knowledge (formulas, techniques, risk factors). Semantically linked to the KG, providing "ground truth" for AI/LLMs.

Core Capabilities Driving Transformation

Modular CACMs

Credit Analysis Capability Modules (e.g., `cacm:SaaS_DefaultRisk_v1`) for reusable, expert-infused analytical building blocks, dynamically composed by the KG.

Advanced Simulation Engine

Models impacts of macroeconomic shifts, industry shocks, and regulatory changes, enabling proactive risk management and "what-if" scenario testing.

AI-Assisted Reporting & Review

LLMs for drafting reports based on CACM outputs and KG context, with augmented review tools for analysts to ensure accuracy and accountability.
